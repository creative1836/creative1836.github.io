Simply put, we want to create potent, positive change.

We are curious, creative students who are determined to change our campus and Atlanta for the better.

Starting in the Fall of 2015, we found each other through friends of friends. We quickly learned that our multidisciplinary experience and education could be a powerful creative catalyst for change on this campus.

So, this is what we understood creativity to be: the belief that a group of seemingly unrelated people can gather to break their own predispositions, discover, and design the most relevant solutions for today’s issues.

We are your design advocate and creative partner. We volunteer to provide any kind of creative services, from designing your logo to leading a purpose discovery session, or even helping you understand the people you are trying to reach.
